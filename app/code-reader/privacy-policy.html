Privacy Policy

English

  • The ML Kit is used for barcode scanning. The ML Kit APIs may contact Google servers from time to time in order to receive things like bug fixes, updated models and hardware accelerator compatibility information. The ML Kit APIs also send metrics about the performance and utilization of the APIs in this app to Google. Google uses this metrics data to measure performance, debug, maintain and improve the APIs, and detect misuse or abuse, as further described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Please check below for detail. https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms

  • Firebase is used to transmit information such as crash and performance to improve the quality of applications. As a result, user information may be collected.

Please check Google's privacy policy for details. https://policies.google.com/privacy
